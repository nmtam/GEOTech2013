Call for Paper

GEOTEC HANOI 2013 Organizing Committee would like to invite scientists and experts, engineers, individuals, organizations domestic as well as foreign to prepare your communication and to participate in this international conference focusing on geotechnics for sustainable development

Conference topics

  1. Foundation for high-rise buildings
  2. Underground projects in soft soil
  3. Soil improvement and reinforcement for infrastructure projects
  4. Monitoring and instrumentation for tunnels and excavations
  5. Geotechnical modeling and numerical analysis

Important dates of the conference

June 10, 2013: Deadline of abstract submission
June 30, 2013: Notification of abstract acceptance and beginning of registration
August 05, 2013: Deadline of full paper submission
September 05, 2013: Notification of full paper acceptance
September 15, 2013: Deadline of registration

Instructions on the format of abstracts & papers of authors

Authors are invited to submit abstract of about 250 - 500 words in English (in MS Word or PDF format), on topics relevant to one of the conference themes.

Abstract of papers should be sent to the conference secretary (secretariat@geotechn2013.vn) before June 10, 2013.

The proceedings including full papers will be provided at the conference.

The official language of the conference will be English

Publication

The conference proceedings will be published by the Construction Publishing House, Ministry of Construction, Vietnam with ISBN. The Proceeding, which Proceedings, which include a hardcopy of final papers and a CD, will be provided to attendants at the conference
